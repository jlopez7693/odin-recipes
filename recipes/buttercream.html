Buttercream Frosting

Description

A delicious buttercream that is both delicious and versatile for any cake decorating needs. Tastes very similar to buttercream used in grocery store bakeries, such as Publix.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces butter (2 sticks)
  • 2 cups vegetable shortening (preferably Crisco)
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp butter flavoring
  • 1 Tbsp meringue powder
  • 2 lbs confectioner's sugar, sifted
  • 1 Tbsp milk (optional)

Directions

  1. Combine the butter, shortening, and salt in a large bowl and beat on low speed for 5 minutes.
  2. Add the vanilla and the butter flavoring and beat until well blended.
  3. Turn off the mixer and add the meringue powder and half of the confectioner's sugar. Mix on the slowest speed the mixer has.
  4. Mix in the remaining sugar 1/2 cup at a time, until all the sugar is mixed in well.
  5. Turn the mixer up to medium and beat until smooth and creamy, about 5 minutes. If the icing seems too thick to spread, beat in up to 1 Tbsp milk to loosen it.

Notes

Make sure to only use gel food coloring when working with buttercream! Water-based food coloring can unintentionally thin out the buttercream.

Add a drop of purple food coloring if you desire a whiter buttercream.
